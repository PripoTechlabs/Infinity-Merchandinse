What types of products can Infinity Merchandise source?

We source across 14 product categories including textiles, footwear, corporate gifts, electronics, machinery, and more. Our global network allows us to find quality manufacturers for any product category you need.

How do you ensure product quality?

We implement rigorous quality control processes at every stage - from manufacturer verification to pre-shipment inspections. Our team ensures all products meet international standards before delivery.

What are your payment terms?

We offer flexible payment terms including LC (Letter of Credit), TT (Telegraphic Transfer), and advance payments. Terms are customized based on order volume and relationship.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary by product category, typically starting from 100-500 units. We accommodate both small businesses and large enterprises.

Can you help with product customization?

Yes, we provide full product customization support including branding, packaging design, and specification modifications to match your requirements.
